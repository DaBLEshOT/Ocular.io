About the project

For the final project for our cyber security class, we chose to build a browser plugin that offers a visual representation of daily analysis of the trackers used across your web surfing.

The plugin directly helps you see which tracker was used most frequently on a certain domain during that specific day.

But because we wanted to showcase more data, we also opted to extend the idea into a web page that allows users to view additional information for longer periods of time, such as weekly and monthly views.

Because we strongly believe in the idea that knowing your weakness makes you less vulnerable, we built our plugin for two of the most commonly used internet browsers: Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome.
